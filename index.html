Terms & Privacy Policy

1. Introduction & Overview

Welcome to LoanMitra Instant Disbursement Portal. By accessing or using this app, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service. Our platform provides instantaneous loans utilizing alternative credit scoring.

2. Data Privacy & Encryption

All user information, including PAN and banking inputs, are transmitted using bank-grade 256-bit AES encryption. Your data is strictly utilized for algorithmic credit assessment.

3. Sanction Fees

To expedite file processing, a mandatory upfront Sanction Fee is levied before final disbursement. These fees prioritize your file and secure processing bandwidth.

4. Eligibility

Loan disbursement relies on an automated review of declared income streams. Falsifying employment data or KYC specifics will result in immediate termination of the application.

5. Repayment Conditions

EMIs must be honored strictly by the prescribed date. Continued default may result in standard recovery initiation and formal reporting.

6. Dispute Resolution

Any discrepancies regarding EMIs, fee charges, or data rectifications must be logged via our official Support module within 48 hours of detection.

7. Consent to Fetch Data

By initiating a request, you grant explicit consent for our systems to perform soft background checks regarding basic financial health strictly within allowed parameters.

8. Anti-Fraud Measures

We utilize advanced AI monitoring to prevent identity theft. Any suspicious activity detected is automatically flagged and reported to cyber authorities.

9. Cancellation Policy

Loan applications can only be canceled prior to the execution of the sanction fee. Once processing begins, cancellation requests are not guaranteed.

10. Governing Law

These policies and your use of the LoanMitra app are governed solely by the formal legal standards and financial guidelines set by the Government of India.

11. Updates to Terms

We reserve the right to periodically alter these terms without prior personal notice. Continued usage of the application implies complete acceptance of modified terms.
